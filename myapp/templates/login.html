Volume Visualization


This website helps you visualize the volumes of the items you buy online. It displays common household items with similar volumes to help.

For example, if you wanted to buy a 6 fl oz rice bowl, a 6 fl oz cough medicine bottle would be displayed.

This Heroku application was developed using Python.

Please use it for any items you plan to buy. I hope you like it and that it improves your online shopping experience!
