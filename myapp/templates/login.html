Volume Visualization


The purpose of this webpage is to help online shoppers visualize the volumes of the items they buy.

For example, a cup of 16 fl oz could be visualized as having the volume of a water bottle.

This application was developed using Python and on Heroku. Please use it for any items you buy. I hope you liked it and found it useful!
